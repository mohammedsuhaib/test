The House

We don't sell square footage. We place lives.

Onyx Estates was founded on a simple refusal: that finding a home should ever feel transactional. Seventeen years later, we remain deliberately small — a house of twelve advisors, each carrying a portfolio they know street by street, door by door.

  • 01
    Private, off-market access

    Over half our placements never reach a public listing. Our clients see them first.

  • 02
    One advisor, start to finish

    No hand-offs, no call centres. The person who opens the first door signs the last page with you.

  • 03
    Architecture-first curation

    We walk every property before we list it. Light, proportion and craft are non-negotiable.

The Journey

Four steps. Zero friction.

01

Discover

A private consultation to understand how you live — then a shortlist drawn from listed and off-market homes.

02

Experience

Unhurried private viewings, at golden hour if you like. We believe a home should be met, not toured.

03

Secure

Negotiation, diligence and paperwork handled by your advisor and our in-house counsel. You stay informed, never burdened.

04

Arrive

Keys in hand — plus introductions to architects, designers and staff who can make the house entirely yours.
